The GREY Score™ Assessment Methodology

Our Proprietary Assessment Framework evaluates your organization across four critical pillars of AI maturity. Each pillar is rigorously analyzed through evidence-based criteria, generating individual scores that aggregate into your comprehensive GREY Score™.

The Four Pillars of AI Maturity

Each pillar represents a critical dimension of AI maturity. Your individual pillar scores illuminate specific strengths and improvement areas, enabling targeted intervention strategies.

G
GOVERNANCE
  • Policy & accountability: Have you published a company-wide AI policy that assigns clear decision rights and oversight responsibilities?
  • Best-practice compliance: How do you routinely benchmark and audit your AI procurement and development processes against recognized frameworks (e.g., NIST, ISO/IEC) to ensure ongoing conformance?
R
READINESS
  • Human capital: What evidence do you have that senior leaders and frontline employees possess the baseline AI literacy required to use and govern AI tools safely?
  • Data foundation: Does your current data architecture (collection, storage, access, quality) fully support rapid model training and deployment, or are there structural gaps that slow you down?
E
EXECUTION
  • Ways of working: Have AI-first practices—prompt playbooks, automated hand-offs, iterative experimentation—been embedded into your everyday workflows, and who is accountable for sustaining that cultural shift?
  • Enablement & feedback loop: Do you give employees access to generative AI tools, deliver sufficient training, and maintain a shared log of AI experimentation stories?
Y
YIELD POTENTIAL
  • Investment resolve: What level of budget and talent flexibility have you committed to close the most critical AI capability gaps over the next 12 months?
  • Value focus: Which high-impact performance gains or cost-reduction opportunities have you quantified and prioritized to capture near-term AI upside?
